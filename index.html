Biography

Dr. Hong Wang is an Associate Professor with School of Mathematics and Statistics at Central South University. He was a visiting post-doc fellow at UCLA. He earned his Ph.D. degree in Statistics from Central South University in 2015. He received dual M.S. degrees in Computer Science from Hunan University, Changsha in 2008.

His research focuses on biostatistics, machine learning, data mining, causual inference, particularly distributed machine learning and survival analysis. His publications have appeared in Statistics in Medicine, Artificial Intelligence in Medicine , Knowledge-Based Systems, Reliability Engineering and System Safety and 30+ SCI journals.
